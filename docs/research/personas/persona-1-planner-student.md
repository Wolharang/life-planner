# Persona 1 — "혼자선 실행이 안 되는 계획형 학생" (본인; primary interview data)

> Part of the LifePlanner customer profile. **Shared foundations (F1–F8), segmentation, POV, and all references
> `[S#]`/interview tags live in [overview](docs/research/personas/overview.md).** Sourced from the user's own 33
> interview answers (`[R1-n]`/`[R2-n]`).

**Identity.** A planner-type (MBTI J) `[R1-17]` who *loves* planning but, alone, loses to evening fatigue.

**Snapshot / context.** Vacation student using the break for **lectures + a project**, **weekend part-time
work** `[R1-2]`. Days are **mostly fixed with small usable gaps** ("점심시간 1시간, 강의 이후 2시간") `[R1-1]`.
**Multi-device**, domestic (KRW), **cost-conscious** (free-only).

**JTBD.** *Functional*: pre-record important events; get an advance alert **on all devices**; for daily plans,
**fix the exact time and alert me so I actually start**; check schedule instantly. `[R1-7,8,16]` — *Emotional*:
pride/relief after doing it ("뿌듯하네") but **minimal next-day carry-over** `[R2-6,7]`; avoid the regret of wasted
time. `[R1-9,R2-8]` — *Social*: mostly none; one external stake — faking an excuse to skip a shift after a clash. `[R1-4]`

**User scenario (설정→발단→행동→결과).** *설정*: 전날, 다음날 21:00 헬스를 정확히 기록. *발단*: 당일 20:xx 알림.
*행동*: "아 맞다 가야하는데… 꼭 가야할까… (4분) 그래도 가야지 오늘 이때 가겠다고 **기록까지 해놨는데**… (준비)
가기 싫은데… 그래도 옷 입었는데 가야지 (2분) 나감." *결과*: ~10분 늦었지만 **성공**. `[R2-1, R1-6]`

**Pains (sharpest first).** ① **Not following through** — "계획을 해도 안 지켜지는 것이기 때문에 해결이 불가"
`[R1-19]`, beaten by evening fatigue/low willpower `[R1-5,15]`. ② **Clashes caught too late** — shift booked over
a concert; faked an excuse, skipped `[R1-4]`. ③ **Scattered, flawed tools** — Kakao (no new-phone sync, not
instantly visible, no offline), calorie app (a chore; forgets kcal → fixed 400/500/400 shortcut; single-device),
budget app (auto-transfers missing → monthly total off, batch re-entry). `[R1-10, R2-9, R2-10]`

**Motivation.** **습득 욕구 (achievement)** — "성과" = concrete output: "헬스장에 감. 필요한 물건을 사옴.
프로젝트를 만듦" vs YouTube = nothing. `[R2-8]`

**Root cause (5Why).** 운동 안 감 → 피곤/의지부족 → 왜 의지로 안 되나 → 혼자만의 다짐엔 **행동을 촉발하는
구조가 없어서** → 정확 시각+알림은 왜 되나 → "지금 안 하면 앞으로도 안 한다"는 큐가 행동을 자동화 `[R2-4,5]` →
**근본: 의도–행동 사이에 실행 의도(구조)가 없음** (동기 부족 아님). `[S1]`

**Current alternatives & why they fail (actual flow, Q4).** Today the user does **not** do D-1 time-block planning
at all — they only pre-record **important events** in **KakaoTalk calendar** weeks ahead (alarms at 07:00 wake, and
**1 hr + 15 min before**), and treat a few key next-day items the same way (as important events). Budget is logged
**immediately after paying**; calories **after eating — but often forgotten/delayed → recorded inaccurately**.
Tools kept only because "더 나은 앱이 없음" `[R1-11]`; left **Naver 가계부** for failing **offline** `[R1-12]`.
→ **Implication: the D-1 time-block "secondary" feature is aspirational, not a current habit — a real adoption risk.**

**Planning moment & hesitation (Q5).** Planning the next day feels **hopeful/expectant** ("이건 꼭 해야겠다… 이렇게
계획했으니 내일은 할 수 있겠지"); but when the next day already feels **overloaded**, they **omit** exercise rather
than over-plan ("넣어도 안 하는 경우가 더 많음").

**What works (from the user's own behavior).** Only **exact time + "must do now" cognition + an alarm** produces
action; vague "오늘 저녁에 운동" does not. `[R1-16, R2-3,4,5]` `[S1]`

**Success / failure signals.** Success = "한 달 동안 설계대로 매일 헬스장 감." `[R1-13]` Failure = "미래 설계를
해도 헬스장을 안 감." `[R1-14]`

**Volume (design sizing).** Important events ~15/mo (알바7+친구3+기타5); daily blocks ~5–6 (fixed 3 + variable 2–3);
expenses ~4/day; meals ~5/day. `[R2-11,12]`

**Fit with LifePlanner.** The core calendar + exact-time execution alarm *is* this persona's lever; low-friction,
offline capture keeps the trackers used.
