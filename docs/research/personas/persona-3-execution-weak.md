# Persona 3 — "착수(initiation)에서 무너지는 자기계발러" (repeating New-Year gym quitter; research-grounded)

> Part of the LifePlanner customer profile. **Shared foundations (F1–F8), segmentation, POV, and all references
> `[S#]` live in [overview](docs/research/personas/overview.md).** This persona is grounded in behavioral-science +
> market research (not direct interview); the user named/refined it as an expansion target.

**Identity.** The **repeating New-Year gym registrant**: signs up full of resolve, goes a few days, then stops —
and does it again next year. Has the goal, can't sustain the effort. Refined by the user. `[R1-13]`

**Snapshot / context (the annual cycle).** The **Fresh Start Effect** (Dai/Milkman/Riis) explains the re-trigger:
New Year is a temporal landmark that files past failure under "old me" and spikes aspiration (+33% exercise at a
week's start), but the boost is emotional and **fades without structure**. `[S22]` The gap is huge: **67% of gym
memberships unused**, **~50% quit within 6 months, ~80% cancel within a year**, only **~8% keep resolutions**;
the emotional high fades **before habit automaticity (~66 days)**. `[S12][S23][S5]`

**Why the cycle repeats (mechanism).**
- **Unsustainable start + fading feeling**: 0→6 days/week → burnout/soreness; motivation built on guilt/feelings
  fades; **fatigue/time** is decisive (**41% too tired after work**); boredom from **no structure/routine**;
  intimidation; cost. `[S23]`
- **One miss → total collapse ("what-the-hell" / abstinence-violation effect)**: a single skipped day reads as
  *complete failure* — "I blew it, I'll try again next year" — triggering abandonment. This is the deep driver
  behind streak-guilt. `[S24]`
- **Achievement goals erode identity**: repeated failure lowers **self-efficacy**; **identity-based**,
  believable-yet-aspirational framing lasts where outcome-only goals don't. `[S25]`

**JTBD.** *Functional*: **cross the starting line** and keep a realistic cadence. — *Emotional*: stop the
"I set goals but never do them" self-image erosion; rebuild **self-efficacy/competence**. `[S25][S4]` — *Social*: mostly private.

**User scenario (설정→발단→행동→결과).** *설정*: 신년, "이번엔 진짜"라며 헬스장 등록. *발단*: 며칠 열심히 → 하루
빠짐. *행동*: "에라 모르겠다, 망했다"(what-the-hell) → 발길 끊김. *결과*: 몇 주 만에 중단, 내년에 다시 반복. `[S23][S24]`

**Pains (sharpest first).** ① **High initiation threshold** (activation energy; intention→action bridge broken;
Zeigarnik). ② **One miss = "what the hell" collapse** (all-or-nothing). ③ **No structure / over-intensity →
burnout & boredom**; fatigue after work. `[S2][S24][S23]`

**Motivation.** Self-improvement, but sustains **only when autonomous, identity-linked, and realistic** — not on
guilt/streaks/heroic intensity. `[S4][S25]`

**Root cause (5Why).** 목표 못 지킴 → 시작·유지를 못 해서 → 왜 → 무리한 강도+피로+구조 없음, 그리고 하루 빠짐을
파국으로 해석(AVE) → 왜 매년 반복 → 신년 랜드마크가 의욕만 주고 구조는 안 줘서 → **근본: 착수·지속을 위한
현실적 구조와 '한 번 빠져도 괜찮다'는 프레이밍의 부재.** `[S2][S24][S22]`

**Current alternatives & why they fail (documented).** Gym membership + willpower + streak apps. Habit trackers
are **abandoned fast** (~90% within 30d) via **streak-guilt** (loss aversion), too many items, vague goals,
avoidance framing; gyms via **unsustainable intensity, fatigue, boredom, intimidation, cost**. `[S13][S14][S23]`

**What works (evidence-ranked).** ① **Temptation bundling** — strongest peer-reviewed evidence (gym visits
**+51%**; decays over time). ② **Exact-time cue / implementation intentions (d≈0.65)** + routine cues. ③ **Realistic
tiny starts (Tiny Habits)** that build self-efficacy. ④ **Identity / approach framing** (believable-yet-aspirational).
⑤ **Never treat a miss as failure** (defuse the what-the-hell effect). `[S15][S1][S16][S25][S24]`

**Success / failure signals.** *Success*: keeps a **modest, sustained** cadence and, crucially, **returns after a
missed day** instead of quitting. *Failure*: another register → few days → quit cycle.

**Fit with LifePlanner.** Exact-time cue + alarm automates the start; **no streak-guilt** and a "**one miss is
fine**" stance directly counter the what-the-hell collapse; **approach/identity framing + realistic blocks** beat
January over-intensity; use the **Fresh Start Effect** to onboard but supply the structure that sustains it.
